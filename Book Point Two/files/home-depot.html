DIY

STUDIO

Turning "someday" furniture into "I built this" furniture.

Campaign Integrated Campaign Deliverables OOH · Browser Plugin · Microsite · Partnerships Year 2025

Home ownership is on the decline. It's hard for young professionals to justify buying the nice, expensive furniture they might want.

The Home Depot's DIY Summer Studio lets you build the furniture you want for cheaper, while adding your own personal touch — turning "someday" furniture into "I built this" furniture.

The Campaign
[ Case Study Video ]
Phase 01
Out-of-Home: Can I Actually Build This?

Transit benches and other outdoor placements are designed to trigger a single question: "Could I actually build this?" A scannable code takes people straight into DIY Summer Studio, where step-by-step guidance and Home Depot materials make the answer feel like a confident "yes."

Phase 02
The Buildability Plug-In

Fans of DIY Summer Studio can download the Buildability Plug-In for their browsers. The extension scans product pages and moodboards across the internet and flags anything that could be turned into a DIY project with The Home Depot.

For each item, the plug-in surfaces a quick snapshot of the build: materials, tools, estimated time — and a direct link to open it as a project inside DIY Summer Studio.

Partnerships

Home Depot will partner with DIY content creators such as Simone Giertz to get their real reactions to our exclusive Summer DIY shed.

Microsite

QR codes lead to the DIY Summer Studio — a site that recognizes photos of furniture and provides users with instructions and a list of materials to build it, available for purchase at The Home Depot.

We didn't just sell materials — we sold the confidence that you could build something worth keeping.
