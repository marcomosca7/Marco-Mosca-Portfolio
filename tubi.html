TUBI

When people hear "free," they assume there's a catch. They assume it's ads — which is true, but boring.

Tubi doesn't believe in boring. So we offered a more ridiculous explanation: something wild enough to distract from the ads, while proving Tubi is the most advanced free entertainment platform on Earth (and off).

Phase 01
Contact

We touched down with the classic ET communication method of choice — crop circles —
earning media coverage and social buzz.

Phase 02
Discovery

A series of billboards documenting the creation of Tubi lets the public in on our process,
assuring them that alien though we might be, our only mission is to provide free streaming for all of humanity.

Phase 03
Transmission Error

It took light-years of research to create a platform with so much free, quality content.
Can you blame our engineers for wanting to take a little credit?

In an oversaturated streaming market, Tubi found a way to stand out — turning consumer skepticism about "free" into a brand moment that reinforced quality, quantity, and, of course, gratuity.

Art Direction — Lauren Trujillo, Nick Alfonso, Annie Ye
