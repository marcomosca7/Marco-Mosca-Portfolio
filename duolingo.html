DUOLINGO

Everything on your phone is designed to steal your attention away from everything else.

Why compete?

We decided to take over the distractions entirely.

The Idea

Every day, Duo the Owl will invade your phone and translate the entire thing into the language you're studying. That means socials, messages, games — everything.

Want your phone back? Just do your damn Duolingo.

Out of Home
Features
Streaksaver

For every hour spent on DuoMode, you'll build a buffer that protects your streak.

DuoBlock

Once you've embraced the spirit of DuoMode, get a DuoBlock to complete your immersive language learning adventure. When scanned, DuoBlock will translate every single device on your home's wifi to the language you're studying.

Instead of competing with the distractions, we embraced them — letting users experience real language immersion in the environment where they're most comfortable: their phones.
