ROOMBA

We were tasked with creating a data-driven campaign.

So using the data already collected by Roomba's camera and sensors, we turned the robot vacuum into the ultimate interior design consultant.

The Idea

Roomba ID's imaging technology recognizes your home's layout and aesthetic, then references its database of similar homes to give interior design recommendations.

Experiential

We partnered with CB2 to create an in-store kiosk that allowed people to design their own space using CB2 furniture recommended by Roomba ID technology.
